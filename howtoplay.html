How to play

What happens and what you do


    Donkey Kong is a game where you climb up ladders or jump onto different platforms
    to get to the top where your objective, a princess, is held captive.
    While climbing, the giant monkey at the top, Donkey Kong, throws barrels at you.
    On some occasions you get to pick up a hammer which allows you to break the barrels which helps you avoid getting hit.
